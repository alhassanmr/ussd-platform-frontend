How it works

From idea to live USSD
in under 30 minutes

01

Create your app

Sign up, enter your company name and pick your gateway — Africa's Talking, Hubtel, or custom.

02

Build your menus

Use the visual menu builder to add options, collect user input, call APIs, and end sessions.

03

Copy your webhook

Paste your unique webhook URL into your gateway dashboard. Takes 30 seconds.

04

Go live

Dial your short code and your USSD app is live. Monitor sessions from your dashboard.

Features

Everything you need
nothing you don't

🧩

Visual menu builder

Drag-and-drop menus with 5 item types — Display, Input, Webhook, Router, and End. No coding required.

🔌

Multi-gateway support

Works with Africa's Talking, Hubtel, and custom gateways. Switch anytime without changing your menus.

🔗

Webhook integration

Call your own APIs mid-session. Look up account balances, process payments, send data to your backend.

📊

Session analytics

See every session, track drop-off points, understand how users move through your menus.

🏢

Multi-tenant

Each company gets their own isolated workspace. Manage multiple USSD apps from one account.

⚡

Variables & personalisation

Collect user input and reference it in later screens using {{variable_name}} syntax.
